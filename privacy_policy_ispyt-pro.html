Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

ByteBuddies built Exam Trainer as an educational test preparation app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Exam Trainer does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, payment information, or other directly identifying personal information.

The app stores your learning activity on your device, including test sessions, selected study packages, question progress, correct and incorrect answer counts, mistake status, and timestamps needed to show your progress and statistics.

How We Use Information

Locally stored progress information is used to provide the app's core features, such as continuing study sessions, showing statistics, tracking mistakes, and improving the order of practice questions.

Local Storage

User progress is stored locally on your device. Educational package content may also be stored on your device so it can be used offline. The app is designed to keep educational content separate from your progress data.

Internet Access

The app may use internet access to load, update, or download educational content and related source materials. Exam Trainer does not use this permission for advertising, analytics, or user tracking.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Exam Trainer does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or social tracking SDKs. We do not sell personal information.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your locally stored study progress with third parties. If you contact us by email, we will use the information you provide only to respond to your message and support request.

Data Retention and Deletion

Progress data remains on your device unless you delete it through app or device controls, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app. Email messages sent to us may be retained as needed to respond to support requests and maintain our records.

Children's Privacy

Exam Trainer is intended for educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures appropriate for a local-first educational app. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted by updating this page and the "Last updated" date above.
